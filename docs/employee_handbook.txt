Version 1.0 | 2025

=======================================================
SECTION 1: LEAVE POLICY
=======================================================

1.1 ANNUAL LEAVE
- All full-time employees are entitled to 12 days of annual leave per year.
- Annual leave must be applied at least 3 days in advance.
- Leave cannot be carried forward to the next year unless approved by management.
- Annual leave is calculated from the date of joining.

1.2 SICK LEAVE
- Employees are entitled to 6 days of sick leave per year.
- Sick leave for more than 2 consecutive days requires a medical certificate.
- Sick leave does not require advance application.
- Unused sick leave cannot be encashed or carried forward.

1.3 MATERNITY LEAVE
- Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave.
- Maternity leave can start 8 weeks before the expected delivery date.
- At least 4 weeks notice must be given before taking maternity leave.

1.4 PATERNITY LEAVE
- Male employees are entitled to 5 days of paid paternity leave.
- Paternity leave must be taken within 3 months of the child's birth.

1.5 UNPAID LEAVE
- Employees may apply for unpaid leave for personal reasons.
- Unpaid leave requires manager approval and HR approval.
- Maximum unpaid leave is 30 days per year.

1.6 LEAVE APPLICATION PROCESS
- All leaves must be applied through the HR system (TeconSolutions portal).
- Leaves must be approved by the direct manager.
- Emergency leaves can be communicated via phone/message and applied retroactively.
- Leave applications submitted after the leave date will be treated as unauthorized absence.

=======================================================
SECTION 2: TIMESHEET POLICY
=======================================================

2.1 TIMESHEET SUBMISSION
- Employees must log their daily work hours in the TeconSolutions portal.
- Timesheets should be submitted every Friday for the week.
- Late submission may affect performance reviews.
- A minimum of 8 hours per working day is expected.

2.2 WORKING HOURS
- Standard working hours: 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM (Monday to Friday).
- Lunch break: 1:00 PM to 2:00 PM (1 hour).
- Effective working hours: 8 hours per day.
- Overtime must be pre-approved by the manager.

2.3 TIMESHEET APPROVAL
- Timesheets are reviewed and approved by direct managers.
- Approved timesheets cannot be modified.
- Rejected timesheets must be corrected and resubmitted within 2 days.

=======================================================
SECTION 3: CODE OF CONDUCT
=======================================================

3.1 PROFESSIONAL BEHAVIOR
- All employees must maintain professional conduct at all times.
- Treat all colleagues with respect regardless of their role or designation.
- Harassment, discrimination, or bullying will not be tolerated.
- Confidential company information must not be shared outside the organization.

3.2 ATTENDANCE
- Regular and punctual attendance is expected.
- More than 3 unexcused absences in a month may result in disciplinary action.
- Habitual late arrival (more than 15 minutes) will be recorded.

3.3 DEVICE AND INTERNET USAGE
- Company devices must be used for professional work only.
- Personal use of the internet should be minimal.
- Downloading or installing unauthorized software is prohibited.
- Company data must be stored on authorized systems only.

=======================================================
SECTION 4: PERFORMANCE REVIEW
=======================================================

4.1 REVIEW CYCLE
- Performance reviews are conducted twice a year (June and December).
- Employees will be rated on task completion, quality, attendance, and behavior.
- Ratings: Excellent, Good, Satisfactory, Needs Improvement.

4.2 INCREMENTS AND PROMOTIONS
- Annual increments are based on performance ratings.
- Excellent rating: 15-20% increment.
- Good rating: 10-15% increment.
- Satisfactory: 5-10% increment.
- Needs Improvement: No increment.

=======================================================
SECTION 5: COMPANY HOLIDAYS
=======================================================

Employees are entitled to all national and company holidays.
The list of holidays is published at the beginning of each year.
Working on holidays requires prior manager approval and compensatory off will be given.

=======================================================
SECTION 6: HEALTH AND BENEFITS
=======================================================

6.1 HEALTH INSURANCE
- All employees and their immediate family are covered under group health insurance.
- Coverage: Up to ₹5 lakhs per year per family.
- Cashless hospitalization at network hospitals.

6.2 PROVIDENT FUND
- PF is deducted as per government rules (12% of basic salary).
- Company contributes an equal amount to the employee's PF account.

6.3 GRATUITY
- Gratuity is payable after 5 years of continuous service.
- Calculated as per the Gratuity Act.

=======================================================
SECTION 7: GRIEVANCE REDRESSAL
=======================================================

- Employees can raise grievances through the HR portal messaging system.
- All grievances will be addressed within 7 working days.
- Grievances can also be raised directly with HR manager.
- Anonymous complaints can be submitted to hr@TeconSolutions.com.

=======================================================
SECTION 8: RESIGNATION AND NOTICE PERIOD
=======================================================

- Notice period for employees: 30 days.
- Notice period for managers and above: 60 days.
- During notice period, employees must transfer all pending work.
- Early release from notice period requires management approval.
- Full and final settlement will be processed within 30 days of last working day.
